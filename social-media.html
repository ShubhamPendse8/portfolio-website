Visol India

Visol India is a pioneer in sustainable solar energy solutions. I collaborated closely with their marketing team to design a vibrant suite of social media creatives that reflected their brand’s mission—clean energy for all.

The campaign aimed to educate audiences on solar panel benefits, state-level subsidies, and installation processes. The designs were optimized for Instagram and LinkedIn, balancing technical accuracy with visual engagement.

  • ✔ 30+ Posts for campaigns, carousels, reels, and grid planning
  • ✔ Theme-based visual strategy
  • ✔ Used Adobe Photoshop + Illustrator for design
