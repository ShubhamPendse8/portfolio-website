My Journey

My first path was Mechanical Engineering. It taught me how to think in systems, understand structure, and break a problem down before trying to solve it.

But over time, I found myself drawn toward creativity and visual communication. I became more curious about how a design could explain an idea, build trust, or make a brand feel memorable.

That curiosity led me to switch into Multimedia and Design. Since then, I have built my career by combining logic, storytelling, and design to create work that feels both thoughtful and practical.
